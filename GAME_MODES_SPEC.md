# Game Modes Specification

## Seven Main Modes

1. Battle Royale
2. Ghost Hunt Squad
3. Team Deathmatch
4. Zombie Survival
5. Race Battle
6. Custom Room
7. Training Ground

## Battle Royale

Large survival mode architecture with local player plus bots first. Supports solo, duo, and squad planning, map zones, loot, vehicles, and final winner logic.

## Ghost Hunt Squad

Flagship mode. Multiple squads enter an India-inspired mystery map, collect clues, complete rituals, face ghost events, and escape through a gate. First design target is 6 squads x 4 players. Later target is 12 squads x 4 players.

Squad roles: Hunter, Mystic, Medic, and Scout.

## Team Deathmatch

5 vs 5 fast match design with respawn, score target, loadout, bot fill, and result screen.

## Zombie Survival

4-player co-op wave mode with waves 1 to 50, boss waves, upgrade station, repair objectives, pickups, revive, and final escape.

## Race Battle

Vehicle-focused mode with racers, bikes, cars, jeeps, boats, boosts, pickups, shortcuts, track hazards, and result screen.

## Custom Room

Create room, join with code, password, choose mode, choose map, player count, squad size, bots on/off, spectator, and tournament room options.

## Training Ground

Practice area for movement, aim, vehicles, ghosts, zombies, tutorial, and sensitivity test.
